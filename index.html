Privacy Policy

Name: The Digital Art Museum (TDAM)

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Digital Art Museum ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and stores your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use TDAM, we collect and store the following information:

  • Account information: username, email address, and encrypted password
  • Profile information: optional profile description and avatar image URL
  • Art preferences: preferred artists, mediums, movements, styles, and time periods collected through the onboarding survey
  • Collections: artwork collections you create, including their titles, descriptions, and privacy settings
  • Saved artworks: artworks you save and add to collections, including artwork IDs and image URLs
  • Artwork views: records of artworks you have viewed within the app, used to personalise your recommendations

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the TDAM experience:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display and manage your personal collections
  • To generate personalised artwork and collection recommendations based on your preferences and interactions
  • To allow other users to view your public collections and profile

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on a privately hosted server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Artwork data displayed in the app is sourced from public APIs provided by The Metropolitan Museum of Art and Harvard Art Museums, and is not linked to your personal information.

5. Public Content

Collections you choose to make public will be visible to other users of the app, including your username and collection contents. You can change a collection to private at any time from within the app.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

TDAM is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
